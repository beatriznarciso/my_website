Why am I a student ambassador? After joining LBS I’ve decided to sign up for it, so prospect students can feel comfortable and at ease to talk to someone who has been to the same process they are now going or hopefully, if convinced by my marvelous testimony, will be going through in the future. It’s also an amazing way to give back to the LBS community, and helping the school maintaining the high level of students always have.
